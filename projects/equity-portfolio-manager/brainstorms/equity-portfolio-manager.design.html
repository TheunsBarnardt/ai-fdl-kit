Regulatory disclosures

Collective Investment Schemes (CIS) are generally medium to long-term investments. Past performance is not necessarily a guide to future performance. The value of participatory interests may go down as well as up. CIS are traded at ruling prices and may engage in scrip lending and borrowing. Annualised returns are period returns re-scaled to a period of 1 year. A schedule of fees and charges is available on request from the Manager.
