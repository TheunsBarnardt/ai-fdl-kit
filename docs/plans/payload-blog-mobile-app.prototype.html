Scaling the authoring workflow

We've been running the content pipeline with two editors for 18 months. At 4 posts a week it works. At 12 posts a week it doesn't — we kept stepping on each other's drafts and losing context between reviews.

Three changes we made:

  • Document locking at the draft level
  • Explicit author field separate from the editor who saved
  • Scheduled publishing with timezone-aware queueing

What we learned
